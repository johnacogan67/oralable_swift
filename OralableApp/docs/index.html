Temporalis · Clinical trial focus

Oralable: Precision
Optical Myography (OMG)

Advanced monitoring of Temporalis muscle activity for Sleep Apnea and Bruxism diagnostics. Structured export, hemodynamic occlusion tracking, and trial-ready reporting.

Download on App Store Learn more →

Features

Everything you need to monitor and understand your muscle activity patterns.

PPG Sensor

Three-wavelength photoplethysmography captures detailed muscle activity data.

Real-Time Display

View your muscle activity metrics live with an intuitive dashboard.

Data Export

Export sessions as CSV files for analysis or to share with providers.

Session History

Review past sessions and track patterns over time.

Bluetooth LE

Seamless wireless connection to your iPhone.

Local Storage

Your data stays on your device. You control sharing.

Clinical 50Hz Sampling

Strict 20 ms grid PPG and motion channels for sleep-study parity and research CSV export.

Hemodynamic Occlusion Tracking

IR-DC trend and AC coupling to capture masseter-adjacent hemodynamic load for bruxism and apnea-related phenotypes.
